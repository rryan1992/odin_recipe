Double Trouble Lemon Chocolate Raspberry Cheesecake Fluff

Delicious low sugar snack for the whole family!

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of zero sugar lemon pudding
  • 1 container of zero sugar cool whip
  • 8 oz whipped cream cheese
  • 2 cups plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup fresh reaspberries

Instructions

  1. Fold in raspberries to your mixture
  2. Mix together well
